Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you:

  • Place an order on our website
  • Create an account
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact our customer service

This may include your name, email address, shipping address, phone number, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your orders
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

3. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information, including SSL encryption for all transactions. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We may share information with third-party service providers (payment processors, shipping carriers) solely for order fulfillment. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at privacy@marthasboutique.com to exercise these rights.
